July Highlights

Saturday, July 4, 2015

With 794 postings (as of this writing), July set a new all-time record for "Who is hiring?", just surpassing April's previous mark of 785. If only every first of the month fell on a business day, there'd be even more job opportunities posted.

Python is the top programming languagea for the third straight month and fifth out the past 6 months, a clear shift from 2014 where JavaScript dominated the top spot.

Mobile is another big winner this month, with both iOS and Android in the top 5. DevOps also makes a big move, as it has become a standard approach and concept for managing software in the Cloud/SaaS world developers live in.
