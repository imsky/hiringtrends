React is the new champ

Sunday, June 18, 2017

Halfway through the year and there is now a new champion, as React becomes the most popular tech skill companies are hiriing for. This is the second victory for React, it also won last November. Python drops to second place after a long string of victories. JavaScript retained its usual third place finish.

Overall, June was a weak start to the summer hiring season, with postings down 15% from May and 5% from last June.
